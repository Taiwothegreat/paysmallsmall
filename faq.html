Frequently Asked Questions

1

When will I collect the item that I am paying for?

Your item will be shipped when you reach the middle of your instalment period and meet all delivery conditions. You must have paid half of the cost of the item in equal instalments.

  • 2-month plan: delivery immediately.
  • 6-month plan: delivery in 3rd month.
  • 12-month plan: delivery in 6th month.
2

What are the requirements?

You need a valid ID, BVN, verified CDcare account, and a steady source of income linked to a Nigerian bank account for auto-debits.

3

Can I cancel my payment plan?

Yes, you can cancel before delivery. Refunds are issued minus a 5% processing fee.

4

What happens if I miss a payment?

Missed payments attract a penalty and may affect your eligibility for future plans. Ensure your account has funds before your due date.

5

Do I need to pay any deposit?

No upfront deposit. Your first instalment counts as your first payment.

6

Is CDcare a loan company?

No, CDcare is not a loan provider — it’s an interest-free instalment payment platform.

7

Can I change my item after paying?

Once a plan starts, you can’t change the product. You can cancel and start a new one instead.

8

Can I buy more than one item?

Yes, but you must complete your first plan before starting another.

9

What items can I buy?

You can buy phones, electronics, laptops, and appliances from verified vendors.

10

Is there interest on payment?

No interest. CDcare lets you pay over time without extra charges.
